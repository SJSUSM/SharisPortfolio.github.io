Mash Up 2

Admittedly, I'm very particular when it comes to watching anime but my friends had introduced me to a new series, Dandadan (2021). After reading the story's plot, I was reminded of the classic meme so this piece is for "if you know, you know" fans. Turbo Granny (The Cat) and the golden "orb" was simple to do but adding the 2 human characters, Ken Takakura and Momo Ayase, to match the meme pose was a bit hard as I was very unfamiliar with the series. After finding the right pose, I was able to crop and edit the picture. The edit is so minor, you can't even see it unless you're familiar of the series / recognize the scene taking place.
